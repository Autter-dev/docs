## How Autter works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the GitHub or GitLab App">
    Connect Autter to your repositories in one click — no CLI setup or configuration required to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Autter reviews every pull request">
    Within seconds of a PR being opened, Autter analyses the diff against your codebase's full history and flags bugs, security issues, and convention violations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Merge with confidence">
    Critical issues block the merge automatically. Lower-severity findings appear as inline comments for your team to review. Clean PRs merge without friction.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Autter is free for public repositories. No credit card required for your first 100 PR reviews on private repositories.
</Note>
